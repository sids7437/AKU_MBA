About School of Management

The Centre for Management Studies under the School of Management, Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna has been established with the vision of providing quality management education and developing future business leaders, entrepreneurs and professionals.

The MBA programme focuses on industry-oriented learning through modern teaching methodologies, case studies, seminars, workshops, internships and skill development activities. The programme aims to develop managerial competence, leadership qualities, communication skills, ethical values and decision-making abilities among students.
