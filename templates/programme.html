MBA Programme

Programme Overview

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) programme at Aryabhatta Knowledge University is designed to develop competent management professionals with knowledge, leadership ability, analytical skills and decision-making capabilities.

The programme focuses on contemporary management practices, industry-oriented learning, research and professional development.

Duration

MBA programme is of 2 years duration consisting of 4 semesters.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
  • Minimum required qualification as per university norms.
  • Admission through prescribed admission process.

Programme Structure

The MBA programme consists of four semesters covering core management subjects, elective courses, practical learning and project work.
